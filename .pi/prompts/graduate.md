---
description: Promote ideas from daily notes to standalone files
---
Scan my daily notes from the past 14 days. 
1. Identify half-formed ideas, recurring "seeds," or significant reflections that deserve their own standalone notes.
2. For each promising idea, propose (or create) a new note that includes:
   - **Core Claim**: The essence of the idea.
   - **Context**: Why it's important.
   - **Connections**: Links to existing notes in the Knowledge Library.

Help me "graduate" my fleeting thoughts into permanent knowledge.
